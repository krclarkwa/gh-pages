From Customer to Done

  1. A Customer phones-in or sends a complaint or a service request.
  2. Based on information from a Customer, County creates a Request (CSR). 
  3. If this Request requires action, Tasks are created and arranged into Work Orders.
  4. Tasks and Work Orders are assigned Resources.
  5. Once Work Orders are complete, Reports can be created.
