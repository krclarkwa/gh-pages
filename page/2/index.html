From Customer to Done

A Customer phones-in or sends a complaint or a service request

Based on information from a Customers, County creates a Request. 

If this Request requires action, Tasks are created and arranged into Work Orders.

Tasks and Work Orders are assigned Resources.

Once Work Orders are complete, Reports can be created.

Terms

Dashboard

Gadgets

Portal frames displaying knowledge, such as progress, work pipelines, KPIs, etc.

Requests

AKA CSRs. Normally, Request is the first step in the workflow.

Work 

Work Orders (projects)

Groups of Tasks, Resources: Labor, Materials...

Tasks

(Work [Order] components) related to Assets

Standalone Tasks

Tasks that are not a part of a Work Order

Non-Asset Tasks

Tasks not related to County-owned Assets and related to Resources and Work

Assets

Tangibles owned by County

Non-Assets

Non-tangibles and tangibles not owned by the County

Work Orders are also Non-Assets

Resources

Time, Labor, Materials, Services, Machinery

Labor Crews

People and Labor Hours

Reports

printables and raw data for Tasks, Work Orders, Assets, Resources, etc.
